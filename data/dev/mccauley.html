I am an assistant professor in the Computer Science Department at Williams College. My research is in algorithms, particularly in data structures and similarity search.

Before coming to Williams I had post docs at Bar-Ilan University, working with Tsvi Kopelowitz and Ely Porat, at Wellesley, and in the Scalable Similarity Search project at IT University of Copenhagen led by Rasmus Pagh. I did my Ph.D. at Stony Brook University, advised by Michael Bender.

My wife Shikha Singh also does computer science, and is an Assistant Professor at Williams College.
