I am an assistant professor of Computer Science at Williams College.

On leave: I am on academic leave from Williams during 2022-2023. During Fall 2022, I will be visiting Benjamin Moseley at Carnegie Mellon University. At CMU, you can find me in Tepper 4203.

My research is in the area of theoretical computer science; particularly in algorithmic game theory and algorithms and data structures for computing on big data.

I obtained my PhD in June 2018 from Stony Brook University. Before that, I completed an Integrated BSc. and MSc. in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.
